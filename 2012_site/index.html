Who's Attending

What is included in the Conference Pass?
Buying a Conference Pass gives you a whole lot more than just having access to the conference, here are some of the additional perks!
  • 3 Nights stay at the Hyatt Mission Bay (Thursday, Friday & Saturday night)
  • Official Valio Con T-Shirt
  • Breakfast & lunch each day
  • Chance to win a bunch of awesome prizes
  • Drink Night: Sample local beer & wine.
  • Chill out at the beach
  • Bonfires with S'mores
  • Fun, Fun, Fun!
What is included in the Double Conference Pass?
All the same benefits as the Conference Pass above. However your Double Conference Pass will be good for two people. The Double Conference Pass is meant for two people attending Valio Con together and who want to share one room. This pass includes just one room for three nights, but gives you two Conference Passes.
Can I get a discount?
We only offer discounts to local San Diego residents. This discount is for locals who do not need to stay overnight at the Hotel, the discounted pass does NOT come with a hotel stay. If you are a local resident, email us for more info. We do not offer student discounts.
Can I book a hotel room elsewhere myself?
No. We have worked out a great deal with the Hyatt Mission Bay and all of our guests must stay there. The price is included in the Conference Pass.
What if I want to stay Sunday night as well?
You can simply ask the Hyatt to extend your stay another night. You will be responsible for the cost of the extra night. Or you can choose to stay elsewhere on Sunday night, it's up to you.
What is the Hyatt Mission Bay like?
Amazing of course! It's an extremely clean and luxurious resort. It's located right on the water (literally) in Mission Bay, San Diego and even has 3 water slides! For more info, visit their website.
What's on the menu?
We plan on having Phil's BBQ, Hodad's Burgers and Fred's Mexican Cafe cook up some great food for us over the weekend. If you have any particular food allergies or food preferences let us know early so we can accommodate you :)
What airport should I fly into?
The San Diego International Airport (SAN). Hyatt Mission Bay is just 5 minutes from the airport.
What time should I arrive on Thursday?
You should plan to arrive at the Hyatt Mission Bay on Thursday afternoon. We will be kicking things off Thursday night at 5pm and you won't want to miss it!
What time should I leave on Sunday?
The conference will continue until 3pm on Sunday. Be sure your flight leaves after that in order to catch the final prize giveaways and event closing!
Twitter Hashtag?
Sure! If you're gonna post things about Valio Con on twitter and want to use a hashtag, use: #vcon
