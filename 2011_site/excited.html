We want YOU in the Valio Con Promo Video!

This is your chance to appear in a promo video for Valio Con!!
All you have to do is film yourself saying why you are excited for Valio Con and send it to us :)

Checkout these two video examples made just to get your creativity flowin'!!!



Video Guidelines

  • Video must be sent to us by February 1st 2011.
  • The video must feature you saying "I'm excited for Valio Con because __________"!
  • Must NOT contain any music, soundtrack audio, or titles/text on the video. Just your voice talking.
  • Must be 8 seconds or shorter in length.
  • Be creative, try getting outside, or away from your computer if possible.
  • When you email us, please include your full name or twitter username so we can add it to the video. If you do not want your name to appear, please say so.
  • Send us your video at the highest possible quality using a file sharing app like Quixly or Cloud App or similar. If your file is small enough to email, you can just email it to us.
  • Have FUN with it & be unique!!!
